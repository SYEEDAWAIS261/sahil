Homemade Margherita Pizza (Italian):
PIZZA is my favourite dish. I like to eat it many times. And I also make it in our home. I would to share a process that how to make a PIZZA in your home.
PROCESS:
  • Ingredients: Pizza dough, tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Instructions: Roll out pizza dough, spread tomato sauce, add sliced mozzarella and basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper. Bake until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
Chicken Stir-Fry (Asian):
this is also my favourite dish. And their is so many dishes which i like to eat it. But this is a special dish according my concept. I also want to share the process that how to make a Chicken stir-fry
PROCESS:
  • Ingredients: Chicken breast, mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, etc.), soy sauce, garlic, ginger, vegetable oil.
  • Instructions: Cut chicken into strips, stir-fry in hot oil with garlic and ginger. Add vegetables, stir-fry until tender. Add soy sauce and serve over rice.
